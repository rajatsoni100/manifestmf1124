SMIMER Hospital

SMIMER stands for Surat Municipal Institute for Medical Education and Research. It is a large 500+ bed hospital attached to a UG/PG Medical college. The Casualty Department is available 24 x 7 where an average of 125+ patients are treated every day. SMIMER established by Surat Municipal Corporation in the year 2000 to fulfill the desire of the people of Surat city & its nearby tribal areas. The institute aims to impart quality medical education to children of the residents of Surat & cater to health care needs to all irrespective of caste, creed, & society
